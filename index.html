About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), advised by Prof. Tao Huang, Prof. Jun Wei, and Prof. Wensheng Dou. My research focuses on graph database systems, with an emphasis on reliability and query optimization.
